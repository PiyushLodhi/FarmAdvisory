Sunflower is a rich source of high quality edible oil (40-43%) and also suitable for edible refined oil and vanaspati because of high degree of polyunsaturated fatty acids, pleasant odour and nutritional value.
Sunflower requires a cool climate during germination and seedling growth.
Sunflower can be grown successfully in any season viz. kharif, rabi and spring throughout India.
Sunflower thrives best on deep loam soils having good drainage and irrigation facilities.
