Brazil is major sugarcane producing country followed by India.
In the tropical region, sugarcane gets more or less ideal climatic conditions for its growth.
A well drained, deep, loamy soil with ample available water holding capacity is considered ideal for sugarcane cultivation.
Sugarcane can be planted by improved method of planting like, deep furrow, trench methods, ring pit method and paired row method instead of furrow system.
Methods like alternate furrow irrigation, drip irrigation and trash mulching could be of use to economize irrigation water during water scarcity periods.
