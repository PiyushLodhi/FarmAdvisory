Groundnut seed contains about 45% oil and 26% protein.
The main groundnut growing states are Gujarat, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, Maharashtra, Karnataka and Rajasthan.
Groundnut can be grown successfully in places receiving a minimum rainfall of 500 mm and a maximum of 1250 mm.
